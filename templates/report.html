Report an Issue

Flag civic problems, infrastructure issues, corruption or service failures through official government grievance portals.

🏙️ Municipal & Civic Issues
📣

CPGRAMS – Public Grievances

Lodge complaints against central govt ministries — roads, water, electricity, waste.

pgportal.gov.in
🚮

Swachh Bharat – Report Garbage

Report open defecation, garbage dumping, and sanitation issues in your area.

swachhbharaturban.gov.in
📞

PM Sampark / Helpline

Reach out for unresolved issues through PM Sampark grievance redressal network.

sampark.gov.in
⚡ Utility Complaints
📡

Telecom Complaint (TRAI)

Report mobile network issues, call drops, data speed problems, spam calls.

trai.gov.in
🛒

National Consumer Helpline

Report consumer fraud, defective products, e-commerce issues. Helpline: 1800-11-4000

consumerhelpline.gov.in
💧

Water & Sanitation Complaints

Report drinking water supply issues, sewage problems to Jal Shakti Ministry.

pgportal.gov.in
⚖️ Corruption & Legal
🚨

Report Corruption (CVC)

File corruption complaint against central government officials with Central Vigilance Commission.

cvc.gov.in
💻

Cyber Crime Portal

Report online fraud, cyberbullying, financial scams. Helpline: 1930

cybercrime.gov.in
📈

Corporate Fraud (MCA)

Report company fraud, investor grievances to Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

mca.gov.in
📌 Important: For life-threatening emergencies call 112 immediately. These portals are for non-emergency civic complaints and may take 30–60 days for resolution.
